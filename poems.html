Cascade

When the universe
pours itself through you,
open your mouth wider
and let it overflow

Flex and reflex

How deep must the flame have scorched
That no cool water could soothe
Nor salve could penetrate

That even then, I would flinch
When the warmth from your skin
Glanced the whiteness of my scars

Geometry

Love is a line
love is triangle
love is a rectangle
pushed at one corner

A love story
with a point
is a coin
with one side

glass bells

walking under
frozen trees
loose leaf ice
rings
glass-bell sweet
beneath my heavy
feet

My heart slows underwater

I did not ask to wake,
but when I do,
I emerge in the quiet
formed from a softness
of breath unhurried

The clarity soon clears
like morning mist
burned by the fires
of a heart in love
and a restless mind

I thank the peace
and prepare for the weight
of the burdens from today,
snagging like sticky burs
that pull down from my center

impulse

when you hear a flie
buzzing
where do you reach?

Leavened

I saw you first
From far away
Captured your image
And ground it into meal

Let yeast and time
Mix with water
Life from life
In the pit of my belly

Live wire

Night never stills in my city.
Its deep and steady current
Hums above and drones below
And between it illuminates
Even the hours bewitched

Lye-slick star

We flee the breath
Of winter's languid sun,
Over crests coerced,
mirrors red and bright;

Through pale gases,
From underearth, above,
Seeps darkling din
Pulled prisms elastic;

Doused salt, twilight slate,
Caught glint of stolen hues,
Until dawn refreshed
Spills ink like amber tar
