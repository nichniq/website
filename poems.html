Cascade

When the universe
pours itself through you,
open your mouth wider
and let it overflow

Flex and reflex

How deep must the flame have scorched
That no cool water could soothe
Nor salve could penetrate

That even then, I would flinch
When the warmth from your skin
Glanced the whiteness of my scars

Geometry

Love is a line
love is triangle
love is a rectangle
pushed at one corner

A love story
with a point
is a coin
with one side

glass bells

walking under
frozen trees
loose leaf ice
rings
like glass bells
beneath my heavy
feet
