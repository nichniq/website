Nick Cassleman - Part One

I'm making a videogame! Right now, it's called Part One. It's an ambitious project with many layers, so I'll start with the big ones:

  • Queer heroism, non-violent adventure
  • Life and nature; miracle and wonder
  • Questions, knowledge, perception
  • Dreams and wyrd time magick
  • Trauma and healing
  • Origins and their growth

My notes are spread far and wide, but I keep an online workspace to collect ideas and inspiration. You're welcome to take a peek!
