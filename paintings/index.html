College oils

I took a painting class in college and learned a bit about painting in oils. I didn't really know how to draw or make art outside my personal cartoon styles, so I relied heavily on abstract. (This has been a common theme in my life, see the stylized figures from Drink n' Draw.)

College watercolor

Watercolors freak me out because I love to overwork things and that's a big no-no with watercolor. Still, a friend asked if I could illustrate a pair of articles for the Southside Weekly magazine after seeing a poster I'd made for taco night watercolor I made for Z&H Market Cafe.
