Nick & Nick

The handle nichniq has a cute little backstory.

Duality has always been a very important concept to me (#libra_sun #gemini_moon). For a while, I really believed in the strong separation between my head and heart; my logic and passion; my science and myth.

At some point, I wanted to find myself a unique handle / moniker / personal brand. Eventually, I came up Nick and Nick, which became Nich and Niq after some YOUTH took nicknick on the Big Platforms. Nich is the logical one (because it looks kinda German and that's the stereotype I associate with Germans) and Niq is the artistic one (the Q stands for QUEER and there's a silent ;) at the end of it).

Technical principles

  • Make it accessible: screen reader support, no JS, navigable on small screens, etc
  • Use semantic HTML tags to encode additional meaning + leverage browser provided styling and controls
  • Make things easy to change, avoid entangling things
  • Only add structure as needed, don't get too fancy too early, write as little code as possible
  • Make the words and the code legible
